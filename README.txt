Collossal Creative Academy — 4‑Page Static Site
================================================

Files
-----
- index.html — Home with hero + auto slider + value pillars + pathway
- about.html — Vision, Mission, Values, Brand Purpose, Archetypes, Color palette
- programs.html — Core programs + Marketing bridge + CTA
- contact.html — Contact & Enrollment form (demo; replace action with your provider)
- style.css — Theme with SA palette (#007A4D, #002395, #FFB612, #DE3831, #000000)
- scripts.js — Mobile menu + auto slider
- /images — put your artwork photos here (update the hero slider to use them if desired)

Brand Content Included
----------------------
- Vision, Mission, Values, Brand Purpose, Archetypes (Creator + Sage), Tone of Voice
- Tagline: "Teach • Reach • Achieve"

How to Use
----------
1) Unzip and upload the files to your host (any static hosting).
2) Replace the `.logo-placeholder` div with your actual logo `<img>` in `header`.
3) Swap in real artwork images: add files into `/images` and update the slider if needed.
4) Hook up the contact form: change `action` on contact.html to Formspree, EmailJS, or your backend.
5) Edit text as you like — all copy is in the HTML files.

Notes
-----
- The header is sticky for easy navigation.
- Colors follow your SA flag palette.
- Design aims to be bold, creative, and accessible.
