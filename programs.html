Programs & Workshops

Learn the craft, create the product, and launch with confidence.

Sketching & Drawing

Foundations in form, proportion, shading, and composition. Build a portfolio-ready piece.

  • Beginner → Intermediate tracks
  • Weekly critiques & guided practice
  • Capstone: Finished portrait

Fabric & Canvas Painting

Techniques across textile & canvas mediums, color theory, and surface preparation.

  • Fabric-safe paints & fixatives
  • Design for wearables & décor
  • Capstone: Sellable canvas or textile piece

Sewing & Product Design

Pattern basics, seams & finishes, machine operation, and quality checks.

  • Beginner tote → advanced accessories
  • Material selection & costing
  • Capstone: Market-ready handcrafted bag

Market-Ready Marketing

Turn creations into income: brand basics, product photography, pricing, and selling online.

  • Shop setup: marketplace or simple site
  • Social & storytelling for creatives
  • Launch checklist & first 10 sales plan

Beauty & Makeup

Techniques for camera-ready looks: skin prep, color matching, and creative styling.

  • Hygiene & kit essentials
  • Natural day → editorial glam
  • Capstone: Portfolio shoot-ready look

Natural Skincare Formulation

Create gentle, effective products using botanical ingredients and safe lab practice.

  • Formulation basics: balms, serums, cleansers
  • Preservation, stability & labeling
  • Capstone: Branded mini skincare set
